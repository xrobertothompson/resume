1: Destination Selection & Budgeting

The first and most crucial part of planning a trip is the destination and planning part;
without considering these, you might find yourself unsure of where you want to travel.
Once you have a destination in mind, it's important to research the best time to visit,
local attractions, and any necessary travel documents. Creating a detailed itinerary
that includes accommodation, transportation, and activities can help ensure a smooth
and enjoyable trip. Additionally, budgeting for your trip and planning for unexpected
expenses will allow you to make the most of your travel experience. Remember,
thorough preparation is key to a successful adventure!
